Why Become A Aggie

What It Mean To Be An Aggie

Being an NCAT Aggie means becoming part of a proud legacy that dates back over a century, rooted in excellence, resilience, and community. As a student of North Carolina Agricultural and Technical State University (NCAT), you are not only stepping into a leading Historically Black College and University (HBCU) known for its innovation in science, technology, engineering, and agriculture, but you are also joining a family that values the pursuit of knowledge and social impact. The Aggie experience is about more than just academic achievement; it’s about developing leadership, embracing diversity, and contributing to a culture that celebrates heritage while pushing forward into new frontiers. The spirit of Aggie Pride is palpable across the campus, from the passionate energy during Homecoming celebrations to the quiet moments of focus in the library. It’s a place where students are encouraged to dream big, work hard, and serve their communities, all while building lifelong connections with fellow students, alumni, and faculty who share a commitment to uplifting each other and achieving greatness. Whether it's excelling in research, representing NCAT in athletic competitions, or creating change in the local and global community, to be an Aggie means to embody resilience, ambition, and a relentless drive to make an impact in whatever field you choose. As an NCAT Aggie, you carry forward a tradition of success that has shaped some of the brightest minds and most influential leaders, and you become part of a movement to inspire future generations to take pride in their potential and continue to reach for excellence.
